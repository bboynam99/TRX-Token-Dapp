Flux is a "Deflationary-Proof-of-Stake" Token deployed on the TRON Network.
This project aims to provide value to holders via future developments of Dapps and games, connected to the Flux Token contract.

Holders have the ability to stake their FLUX, and earn more tokens as FLUX is used by others.
The earnings from staking will vary, proportionate to tokens staked.

Deflationary Proof-of-Stake is the combination of two popular kinds of token.
Flux achieves this by burning 1% of every Unstake, removing it from the Total Supply. Transfers do not incur any burn, only unstaking.
Flux is also Proof-of-Stake token, enabling holders to lock up their tokens for a period of time, to earn more tokens.

At the moment, Flux is still under development. Dapps and games may be added in the future, which would feed more earnings to those who have staked Flux tokens. Bear in mind, Flux is an experiment - meaning at any time, just like any other project, it could suddenly become valueluess. Please ensure you are only using assets you can afford to lose.
